Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and cash on delivery.

Do you deliver?

Yes, we offer delivery services to our customers.

Which area do you cover for delivery?

We deliver to all areas within Semenanjung.

Do you offer any gluten-free, dairy-free, vegan, or sugar-free items?

Yes, we have a variety of options available for customers with specific dietary requirements. Please check our menu for more details.

How far in advance should I place my order?

It's recommended to place your order at least 2 Days in advance to ensure availability.

How should I store my baked goods?

To maintain freshness, store our baked goods in an airtight container at room temperature or in the refrigerator.
